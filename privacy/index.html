1. Information We Collect

YouTube Analyzer by Norcanto collects minimal data to provide our service. We collect:

(c) cookie consent preferences stored locally in your browser.

Information is used to perform channel analysis, improve our estimation algorithms, monitor service performance, and serve relevant advertising via Google AdSense where applicable.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your theme and language preferences, cache channel analysis results for 24 hours, and serve advertising. You may opt out of non-essential cookies via our cookie consent banner.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the YouTube Data API v3 (subject to Google's Privacy Policy), Google AdSense for advertising, and may use analytics services. These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

5. Data Retention

Channel analysis results are cached in your browser's local storage for up to 24 hours. We do not store personal information on our servers beyond what is necessary to deliver the service.
