Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Norlytics collects minimal data to provide our service. We collect: (a) YouTube channel URLs you submit for analysis; (b) anonymous usage data such as page views and feature interactions via analytics; (c) cookie consent preferences stored locally in your browser.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information is used to perform channel analysis, improve our estimation algorithms, monitor service performance, and serve relevant advertising via Google AdSense where applicable.

3. Cookies

We use local storage for theme, language, consent preferences, and short-lived channel analysis caching. Google Analytics and Google AdSense scripts load only after you select Accept in the consent banner. Selecting Reject keeps those non-essential Google scripts disabled.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the YouTube Data API v3 to retrieve public channel statistics. With your consent, we also use Google Analytics to understand site usage and may use Google AdSense to serve advertising. These Google services are subject to Google's own terms and privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

Channel analysis results are cached in your browser's local storage for up to 24 hours. We do not store personal information on our servers beyond what is necessary to deliver the service.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. Contact us at privacy@norcanto.com to exercise these rights.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@norcanto.com.
