YouTube Niche Trends Dashboard

Analyze a recent sample of public YouTube videos to understand momentum, competition, engagement, and content opportunities.

Analytics use up to 50 recent relevant public videos from the last 90 days. No fake analytics are generated.

How to Use the Dashboard

Follow these steps to turn recent public YouTube data into a practical content plan.

1

Enter a niche, topic, or keyword

Start with a clear search such as “fitness,” “Christian motivation,” “AI tools,” or “football highlights.”

2

Understand the analysis period

The dashboard compares recent public YouTube activity from the last 7, 30, and 90 days when enough data is available.

3

Read the key scores

Trend Score shows momentum, Competition Score estimates how crowded the sample is, and Opportunity Score highlights promising gaps.

4

Review the analytics

Compare average views, engagement, upload frequency, fast-performing videos, and common keywords to understand the niche.

5

Use the AI suggestions

Use the generated title ideas, tag suggestions, video angles, and niche summary as creative starting points.

6

Turn insights into content

Use the evidence to decide what videos to create and how to differentiate them. Do not blindly copy other creators.

Scores are directional indicators based on a recent public-data sample, not guarantees of future performance.
