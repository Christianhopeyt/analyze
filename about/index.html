About YouTube Analyzer by Norcanto

YouTube Analyzer by Norcanto is a free tool that allows anyone to estimate the revenue, growth potential, and performance metrics of any public YouTube channel.

Our Mission

We believe content creators and marketers deserve transparent, data-driven insights into YouTube's monetization ecosystem. Our tool democratizes access to revenue intelligence that was previously only available to agencies and large media companies.

How We Calculate Revenue

Our revenue estimations are based on a proprietary algorithm that factors in the channel's detected content niche, primary audience geography, content type (long-form vs. Shorts), average view counts, and real-world RPM benchmarks gathered from industry research. We display a minimum, average, and maximum range to accurately reflect the inherent variability of YouTube advertising revenue.

Data Sources

Channel statistics are retrieved via the official YouTube Data API v3. Revenue figures are estimates only and are not provided by or affiliated with YouTube or Google.
